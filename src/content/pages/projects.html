Kitchen Remodeling

Martinez Family Kitchen - Half Moon Bay

A full kitchen modernization that transformed a cramped, outdated space into an open, bright heart of the home. The work preserved the house's architectural character while upgrading systems, finishes, and function.

The Challenge

The Martinez family had lived with a small, inefficient kitchen for over 15 years. With two teenagers and frequent entertaining, they needed a durable, spacious layout that supported everyday family life and gatherings.

Project Details

  • Removed a non-structural wall to achieve an open-concept layout
  • Installed custom white shaker cabinets with soft-close drawers
  • Upgraded to quartz countertops with a waterfall-edge island
  • Relocated plumbing and electrical to optimize the work triangle
  • Added recessed and pendant lighting throughout
  • Installed luxury vinyl plank flooring for durability and aesthetics
Timeline: 7 weeks Location: Half Moon Bay, CA
