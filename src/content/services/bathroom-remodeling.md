## Bathroom remodeling that performs and delights

We handle everything from layout and waterproofing to tile selection and fixture installation. Our process ensures wet areas are built to last and look exceptional.

### Services included

- Full demo and waterproofing
- Shower/tub system installation
- Custom vanities and storage solutions
- Lighting and ventilation upgrades
- Accessible design and grab bar planning

### Project approach

Clear milestones, quality subcontractors, and thorough inspections keep projects on schedule and within scope. We provide material lists and finish samples during the design phase.

### Why clients choose us

1. Comprehensive planning — we address layout, storage, lighting, and ventilation.
2. Quality materials — we specify durable, water-resistant materials for longevity.
3. Skilled craftsmanship — our team ensures precise installation and finishing.

### Typical project phases

1. Initial consultation and design
2. Material selection and budgeting
3. Permit acquisition and scheduling
4. Demolition and structural work
5. Plumbing, electrical, and HVAC modifications
6. Waterproofing and tile installation
7. Fixture installation and final walkthrough

### Design considerations

- Optimize layout for space and function
- Select moisture-resistant materials and finishes
- Incorporate universal design principles for accessibility
- Plan for adequate lighting and ventilation

### Example scope options

- Guest bath remodel: new fixtures, tile, and lighting
- Master bath renovation: custom shower, dual vanities, and luxury finishes
- Universal design update: barrier-free shower, grab bars, and adjustable fixtures

We’ll work with you to create a bathroom that meets your needs and exceeds your expectations.
