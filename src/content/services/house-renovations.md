## Transform your home with comprehensive renovation services

House renovations require vision, precision, and coordination. Whether updating an older home's systems and finishes or reconfiguring layouts for modern living, we handle every aspect — from initial design concepts through final punch list and warranty.

### What we do

- Whole-home and multi-room renovation planning
- Structural modifications: walls, beams, floor systems
- Complete electrical, plumbing, and HVAC upgrades
- Insulation, drywall, and plaster repair
- Flooring, trim, cabinetry, and finish work
- Exterior improvements: siding, windows, doors

### Why clients choose us

1. Experience with older homes — we understand hidden conditions and adaptive solutions.
2. Clear documentation — detailed scopes, allowances, and change order processes.
3. Quality assurance — inspections at key milestones and final walkthrough.

### Typical project phases

1. Design consultation and scope definition
2. Permit preparation and approvals
3. Demolition and structural work
4. Rough-in: electrical, plumbing, HVAC
5. Insulation and drywall
6. Finish carpentry, flooring, and painting
7. Final inspections and client handoff

### Design considerations

- Address deferred maintenance early: roofing, foundation, drainage
- Upgrade insulation and windows for energy efficiency
- Plan electrical capacity for modern appliances and technology
- Consider open floor plans while respecting structural limitations
